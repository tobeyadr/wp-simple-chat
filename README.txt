Say hello to the easiest way to add Facebook Chat to your website.

== Description ==

Having chat on your site is paramount to providing a quick and cohesive customer experience for your visitors.

By being able to chat with customers you can answer their questions in real time, and provide useful tips an insights without them having to find it on your site.

You can use this plugin as long as you have a Business Facebook Page, and Facebook's chat service is 100% free, like this plugin.

This plugin has a direct connection to Facebook and doesn't require you to sign up for any third party service. All you need to to is provide your Facebook Business Page ID and you can start chatting with visitors.

We currently do not provide any premium version of this tool, we just hope that you find it helpful.
